NOTE

1. log10(x)
  let x be a number
  then the value of log10(x) will give no of digits - 1
 
EXAMPLE:
  x = 12345
 log10(12345) = 4 = no. of digits - 1

2. n%10
  let n be a number
  then the remainder when n is divided 10 gives the last digit of the number

EXAMPLE:
  n = 5625
  5625 % 10 = 5 = last digit
